How Game 1 Works:

You will be paired randomly and anonymously with another participant. In this game, one of you will be Participant 1 and the other Participant 2. Prior to making a decision, you will learn your role, which will be randomly assigned. There are {{ Constants.endowment }} to split. Participant 1 will decide how much she or he will retain. Then the rest will go to Participant 2. So the more Participant 1 keeps, the less Participant 2 gets .

In addition, each player will also be randomly allocated an initial endowment which will either be 10, 100, or 300 points. Each player has an equal probability of receiving each of the initial endowments, and your initial endowment is yours to keep regardless of the income split decision. You will learn what your initial endowment is before the game starts, as well as the endowment of your anonymous partner.
